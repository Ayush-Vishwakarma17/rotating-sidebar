Sony’s best-selling PS5 controller is the DualSense wireless controller, widely acclaimed for its innovative features and ergonomic design. The DualSense stands out with its advanced haptic feedback, providing players with realistic tactile responses that mirror in-game actions, and adaptive triggers that offer dynamic resistance for more immersive experiences—whether you’re drawing a bowstring or accelerating a car. Its streamlined, comfortable shape caters to gamers of all hand sizes, making it ideal for both intense multiplayer sessions and casual play. Additionally, the built-in microphone and speaker enable seamless in-game communication, while the varied color options allow users to personalize their setup. For most players, the DualSense strikes the ideal balance of innovation, reliability, and value, consistently topping lists as the standard by which other PS5 controllers are measured
